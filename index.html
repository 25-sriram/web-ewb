ABOUT US

Engineers Without Borders (EWB) is a global network of non-profit organizations that unite engineers, professionals, and students to address critical challenges. They apply engineering expertise to improve living conditions in underserved communities.

MISSION

Mission: The mission of Engineers Without Borders (EWB) at SVCE is to utilize engineering as a catalyst for positive and sustainable global change. Our multifaceted mission includes community-centric engineering, education, and skill development, fostering global citizenship, promoting innovation and research, and actively seeking collaboration and partnerships.

VISION

Vision: We envision a future where engineering is a force for positive change, empowering communities, fostering cross-cultural understanding, and addressing the most critical issues facing humanity. Through education, collaboration, and ethical engineering practices, we strive to build a network of socially responsible engineers dedicated to improving the well-being of people around the globe.
