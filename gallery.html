23rd Aug 2025
National Space Day

The EWB-SVCE Student Chapter celebrated National Space Day with a guest lecture by Mr. Paul Savio, who spoke about the trillion-dollar growth of the space industry and the role of AI/ML, robotics, and innovation. He emphasized interdisciplinary skills and career opportunities beyond aerospace. The session motivated students to explore space as a career avenue and contribute to future global advancements
