FireSat

The Fire-Watching Eyes in the Sky

What if Satellites Could Spot Wildfires?

Imagine if satellites could detect a wildfire just minutes after it starts—helping save lives, homes, and forests. That’s exactly what FireSat is built for.

What is FireSat?

FireSat is a satellite system that uses smart sensors and AI to detect wildfires from space.

  • Early Detection – Detects small fires rapidly using sensors.
  • AI-Powered – Machine learning confirms real fires using heat patterns.
  • Built for Safety – Enables quicker response, preventing fire spread.

Amazing Facts

  • Infrared Sensors – Detects heat even in darkness or smoke.
  • Global Coverage – Scans Earth every 20 mins with 50+ satellites.
  • AI Analysis – Identifies wildfires and risks.
  • Rapid Alerts – Sends alerts to responders in minutes.
  • Collaborative Effort – Built by Google Research, Muon Space & Earth Fire Alliance.

How Do People Use FireSat?

  • Emergency Alerts – Provides early warnings to fire departments.
  • Live Maps – Aids in rescue planning with real-time data.
  • Climate Study – Helps scientists analyze wildfire patterns.

Why It Matters

  • Wildfires burn millions of acres every year.
  • Fast detection prevents destruction, protecting nature & animals.
  • FireSat offers a new way to fight fire—from space.
