EWB Events

2022 - 2023

28th April 2022
SynergICE An Inter-College Techno-Management Workshop

The SynergICE Workshop, organized by EWB–SVCE with IEEE, CSI, and EPIC, took place on April 28–29, 2022, with 221 participants from 14 branches. The sessions covered product development, marketing, and finance, guided by industry experts. The highlight was “Pitcher Perfect,” where teams pitched business strategies, and the event ended with awards and active participation

14th Mar 2022
Infinitus'22

INFINITUS 2K22, a five-day event by EWB–SVCE, showcased sophomores’ analytical, technical, and creative talents through contests like Quiz-a-Thon, Battle of Wits, Dramaketrix, Pitch Perfect, and Strategica. With enthusiastic participation, the series concluded with Gokul Krishnan from Mechanical Engineering winning the Rolling Trophy and certificates awarded at the valedictory

5th Feb 2022
Inaugration Ceremony

The EWB–SVCE Inauguration 2022 was held virtually on February 5, 2022, with 98 participants on Google Meet. The event introduced the newly elected board members and highlighted the chapter’s activities for the year. Chief Guest Shri R. Elango delivered an inspiring lecture on career growth and the role of engineers in addressing societal needs. The session concluded with a vote of thanks and the National Anthem
