EWB Events

2022 - 2023

23rd Jun 2023
All India Meet (AIM23)

The EWB All India Meet 2022–23 was held on June 23–24, 2023, at GITAM University. SVCE students presented a low-cost carbon absorber project and won First Prize in paper presentation.
8th May 2023
Agronomics

AGRO-NOMICS, held with Vapour Farm, challenged teams to solve agriculture problems with sustainable solutions. Winning teams received awards and ₹5000 cash prize.
20th Feb 2023
Infinitus '23

INFINITUS ’23 featured Wordsmith, Block and Tackle, Mockup, Shipwreck, and Strategica. Mukundan (Chemical Engg.) emerged winner and received the Rolling Trophy.
10th Nov 2022
Ideathon '22

Ideathon’22 saw 18 teams showcase innovative ideas. Team CREO won, followed by TRIOZ as first runners-up. Judges praised creativity and problem-solving skills.
22nd Sep 2022
Inauguration Ceremony

The 2022–23 inauguration with 117 participants introduced new board members and featured Chief Guest Mr. Alagu Sundaram Veerappan, inspiring future EWB initiatives.
