EWB Events

2022 - 2023

23rd June 2023
All India Meet (AIM23)

The EWB All India Meet 2022–23 was held on June 23–24, 2023, at GITAM University, Hyderabad, with keynotes on education, clean water, gender equality, and innovation. EWB–SVCE students presented their project on low-cost carbon absorbers and won First Prize in the paper presentation, marking a proud achievement for the chapter

8th May 2023
Agronomics

AGRO-NOMICS, organized by EWB–SVCE with Vapour Farm, was an innovation challenge addressing agricultural problems. Teams presented sustainable and creative solutions before a jury, with the best ideas receiving awards and a cash prize of ₹5000. The event fostered innovation, collaboration, and practical approaches to farming challenges

20th Feb 2023
Infinitus'23

INFINITUS ’23, the five-day flagship event of EWB–SVCE, was designed for sophomores to showcase their analytical, technical, and creative skills. Events like Wordsmith, Block and Tackle, Mobile App Mockup, Shipwreck, and the finale Strategica tested participants in problem-solving, teamwork, and strategic planning. The event concluded with Mukundan from Chemical Engineering emerging as the winner and receiving the Rolling Trophy
10th Nov 2022
Ideathon'22

Ideathon’22, organized by EWB–SVCE on November 10, 2022, provided a platform for students from diverse domains to showcase creativity and problem-solving skills. With 18 teams and 36 participants, the event featured innovative ideas evaluated by expert judges. Team CREO emerged as winners, followed by TRIOZ as first runners-up.The competition highlighted innovation, collaboration, and practical solutions across engineering disciplines
22nd Sep 2022
Inaugration Ceremony

The EWB–SVCE Inauguration 2022–23 was held on September 22, 2022, with 117 participants at the Function Hall. The event introduced newly elected board members and presented the annual report of 2021–22. Chief Guest Mr. Alagu Sundaram Veerappan shared insights on past and future EWB initiatives, while core team members were felicitated. The ceremony concluded with a vote of thanks and the National Anthem
