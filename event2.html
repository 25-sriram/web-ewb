EWB Events

2023 - 2024

30th April 2024
Guest Lecture

EWB–SVCE hosted an online guest lecture on “Strategies for Mitigating Noise Pollution” on April 30, 2024. Mr. Ritesh Prasad Gurung from the Central Pollution Control Board discussed health and environmental impacts, practical solutions, and sustainable approaches. The session engaged 83 participants and emphasized collaborative efforts for a healthier future

5th Feb 2024
Infinitus'24

INFINITUS ’24, a five-day flagship event by EWB–SVCE, engaged sophomores in cryptograms, brain teasers, ad surfing, time-travel debates, and a stock market challenge. The contests tested creativity, problem-solving, and presentation skills, with active student participation throughout. The event concluded with a valedictory session, where Srimathi R won the Rolling Trophy

12th Oct 2023
Inaugration Ceremony

The EWB–SVCE Student Chapter Inauguration for 2023–2024 was held on October 12, 2023, introducing new members and outlining plans under the motto “Ideate, Innovate, and Implement.” With 68 participants, the event featured an inspiring address by Chief Guest Dr. R. Muthucumaraswamy and concluded on a note of optimism and unity
2nd Sep 2023
ISRO Visit

On September 2, 2023, EWB–SVCE members witnessed the launch of Aditya-L1, India’s first solar observatory mission, at ISRO Sriharikota. Seventeen students attended, gaining insights into rocket technology and India’s space progress. The visit was both educational and inspiring, fostering curiosity and passion for STEM
